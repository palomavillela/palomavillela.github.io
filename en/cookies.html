Cookie policy

Cookies are small text files that are stored by web sites in your browser to be able to offer you a better user experience, and sometimes to be able to identify your session and thus study your browsing patterns.

In ashtanga.padmaworld.com we believe it's important to protect your privacy and we don't use any cookie that can identify you nor do we study your browsing patterns. In fact, we only use one single cookie that stores your language preference, so that you don't need to select the language every time you visit us.

Nevertheless, there are some pages in this site that contain Google iframes. We use Google iframes because they make several site functionalities much more easy.

  • Ashtanga classes >> Timetable (http://ashtanga.padmaworld.com/en/ashtanga/timetable.html). This page contains an iframe with a Google calendar.
  • Ashtanga classes >> Location (http://ashtanga.padmaworld.com/en/ashtanga/location.html). This page contains an iframe with a Google map.
  • Contact (http://ashtanga.padmaworld.com/en/contact.html). This page contains an iframe with a Google form.
It's possible that those iframes use cookies from Google, on which we have no control. For more information, visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies?hl=en-EN.
