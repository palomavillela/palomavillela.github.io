Privacy notice

For any doubt, question or complaint, please contact us. You'll find our contact information in http://ashtanga.padmaworld.com/en/contact.html.

多What data do we collect from our users?

We only collect the data provided by our users when they fill in and submit any of our forms. We have 2 types of forms:

  • A contact form on our web site (http://ashtanga.padmaworld.com/en/contact.html). This form collects 4 pieces of information: Your email, the subject of the message, the message itself and whether you wish to receive news on PadmaWorld classes and events. This is the only form on our web site.
  • We also use forms so people who wish to participate in our workshops can register. These forms collect the following pieces of information: Your name and surname, your email, your phone number (optional) and possibly the answer to some question related to the event in question, for example: What is your practice level?

多Why do we collect this data and what use do we make of it?

To be able to answer your questions, to be able to inform you of news about PadmaWorld classes and events, and to be able to register event reservations and manage such events. We'll never use your data for other goals but those.

多How do we store your data?

We store it using Google Forms system. This means that your data is protected inside our Google account. Only those with the credentials of our team will be able to access the data. For more information about Google's privacy policy, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en-EN.

多Can you claim your data or ask us to delet it?

Yes. You can exercise your rights as stated in the General Data Protection Regulation (https://gdpr-info.eu), which include, among others: access your data, correct your data and delete your data. To execute any of those actions, please contact us. You'll find our contact information in http://ashtanga.padmaworld.com/en/contact.html.
